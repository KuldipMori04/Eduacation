Why Study Short-Term Courses after the 10th?

There are numerous advantages of studying short-term courses after class 10th. Some of these are mentioned below:

  • They take less time to complete. You can spend the remaining time learning new skills or applying them practically.
  • It provides job training experience.
  • It equips students with industry-specific skills and helps them.
  • It helps the candidates to explore diverse career options.
  • It provides a range of employment opportunities to students.
  • A viable option for financially unstable students.
  • It helps in catering to soft skills and professional skills.

The Need For Career Counseling After 10th

“Science, Commerce or Arts?”, the question every 10th standard student is stumbled upon after completing their board examinations. Choosing your career line after 10th standard can be daunting as assessing one’s strengths and weaknesses is not an easy process. With the ever-increasing degree courses and job profiles, the process further becomes complex. Thus, the need for support and guidance at this important junction of life is necessary. Career counselling after 10th plays a pivotal role in shaping up the career of students. In this blog, we will talk about the need for career counselling and where to get the best guidance. But before that, let us get an overview of the streams you can choose after secondary school.

What are the Career Options After 10th?

  • Science

    – Deemed as one of the most favourite streams, Science offers a wide range of lucrative career options in the fields of Medical Science and Engineering. The biggest advantage of choosing the Science stream is that you can switch to any other career line in case the job profile does not match with your professional goals.

  • Commerce

    – The 2nd most popular stream after 10th is Commerce. For those of you who are interested in learning about Finance, Accountancy, Business Law, and Economics, this is the right stream to kickstart your career in. This stream also offers an array of career opportunities to those who pursue professional courses like the Financial Analyst(offered by the CFA Institute), CA, and CS which incidentally are also some of the highest salary jobs for commerce students.

  • Arts/Humanities

    –This stream is considered as one of the most exciting and off-beat choices after class 10th. With different industries related to this stream developing at a rapid pace, more students are now opting for this stream. Formerly known for offering research-based career options, the Arts stream subjects now prepare students for numerous, un-conventional opportunities.
