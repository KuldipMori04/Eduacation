BCA Course Details

What is BCA?

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 3-year undergraduate course dealing with computer applications and information technology subjects like C++ and JAVA, Networking, Data Structures, Statistics, Numerical Techniques, and Algorithm Design ensuring it is at par with BTech/BE courses in the areas of computer application and IT.


BCA Full form is Bachelors’s in Computer Application. GPRS Full Form is General Packet Radio Service, SAS Full Form is Statistical Analysis Software, HTTP Full Form is Hypertext Transfer Protocol. BCA Syllabus deals with the world of programming languages and prepares for a professional life in Information Technology.

Why Study BCA Course?

There are plenty of reasons to study BCA. There is an utmost need for skilled professionals to serve and solve the various problems in the world of technology and IT in India. Most of the students prefer to study this course because of the following advantages:


  • The BCA programme opens up a variety of employment options, particularly in the IT industry.
  • BCA is almost equivalent to the BTech course. The major benefit of opting for a BCA course is that it is a 3-year program whereas BTech is a 4-year course.
  • With a significant push of INR 88,567 crores investment in 2022–2023 by the GOI, the IT industry is at the forefront of everything, and students with a background in computer applications will have plenty of options.
  • A handful of colleges offer BCA specialization as well in the field of Data Science, Data Analytics, and Computer Science. Check: Online Computer Courses
  • Gartner projects that NET IT spending increased by 24% from the year 2021 to USD 101.89 billion in 2022.
  • There is an opportunity for students to go for higher studies too like MCA, to add an extra edge and update their skills & experience, which leads to career development.
  • Students become proficient in programming languages such as JAVA, C++, Python, CSS, Linux, etc., to enhance their technical skills as well.
  • A BCA graduate is in high demand since the requirement for good, skilled professionals and experts to operate and function is there as systems continue to supplant human labour.
  • In terms of salary, BCA graduates can earn INR 2.4-5 LPA in the starting and after getting industry experience and becoming proficient in advanced technical skills, the salary hike can be around 30-40% and you can earn around INR 3.5-8 LPA after 2-4 years of experience. Read BCA Jobs & Salary to know more.

BCA Course Benefits

  • Three years of BCA coursework dedicated to the study of computers and information technology (IT) paves the way for the development of knowledge, skills, and a career in disciplines associated with IT.
  • Numerous work prospects in the IT and computer fields are offered by the BCA programme.
  • Graduates of BCA programmes can find employment in both the public and private sectors.
  • BCA places little to no emphasis on the hardware and is mostly a software-oriented training. As a result, it requires no physical effort from you and offers a stress-free working environment.
  • BCA courses are available in a number of specialities, including BCA Computer Science. For the students, BCA Data Science, etc.

BCA Admission: Entrance-Based

Students are required to appear for an entrance exam followed by the counseling process or Personal Interview Round after qualifying.
