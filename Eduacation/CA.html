CA Course Details after 12th

CA course is among the most opted professional courses in India. Thousands of students enrol in this course every year to become Chartered Accountants. If you’re also registering for the Chartered Accountancy course and want to know everything about it, this is the only guide you’ll need.

In this guide, you’ll get all the CA Course details such as fees, syllabus, eligibility, duration, training, salary, books, and more.

The ICAI is the governing body that conducts the CA exams. Recently, it has proposed a new scheme of education and training for the CA Course. To know all the details about the CA new scheme 2023, visit the page through this link.

What is a CA?

Chartered Accountant or CA is a designation given to an accounting professional responsible for preparing, auditing and managing the financial books, offering insights & advice that help in the business growth, ensuring compliance with the taxation work, etc. To become a Chartered Accountant in India, you must pursue the CA course. The major job profiles of a CA are Accountant, Financial Analyst, Auditor, Senior Executive, etc.

Levels of the CA Course

The CA Course has three levels:

1. CA Foundation
2. CA Intermediate
3. CA Final
In addition, students also have to complete the CA articleship training of three years. So, after passing these three exams and completing the practical training, you will become a CA.

CA Course Duration for Foundation, Inter and Final

Students are excited to know in how many years they can add the prefix CA before their names. Hence, the CA Course duration after the 12th is five years, whereas the duration after graduation is 4.5 years. The duration of each level is:

1. CA Foundation: The total duration of the CA Foundation Course is 6 months, where the study period is for 4 months and the waiting period is of 2 months

2. CA Intermediate: For CA Intermediate Course, the study period is 8 months, and candidates need to wait 2 months for the CA Inter results. Hence, the total duration of the Intermediate will be 10 months.

3. Articleship Training:The total duration of the CA articleship training is for 3 years.

4. CA Final: The study period for CA Final Course is 6 months. Furthermore, the CA Final result will be declared after 2 months; thus, the total time period will be of 8 months. However, students can also study for their CA Final exams while doing the articleship training and give the exams earlier.
