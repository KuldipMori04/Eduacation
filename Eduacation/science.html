What is the career option after the 12th for science students?

There are many students who are in dilemma that what are the career option after they completed their courses in the science stream. So to clear their all doubts we have created a list that they can seek after the completion of their course.

1. Engineering

Those who have completed their courses in Engineering can apply to various MNCs as per the branch. There is a number of companies like TCS, Wipro, IBM, Infosys, and others which are offering jobs to young and fresh talent, and by being a part of such reputed MNCs they can excel in their career to the new pinnacle.

2. Bachelor of Science

On Completing your bachelor’s with a science stream you can work as a researcher, teacher, lab assistant, etc.

3. Bachelor of Computer Application and Computer Science

On Completing this course you can apply for jobs in top IT companies in India, tech support, BPOs, etc.

4. Bachelor of Architecture

After this, you can apply for landscape, construction, and design architecture.

5. Bachelor of Planning

This course can open various gates such as urban development department, as a planner in the tourism department, or environment planning.
